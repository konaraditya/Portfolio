About Me

THE GUY BEHIND THE TIMELINE

Hey, I'm Aditya a video editor based in Mumbai. In the last 1.5 years, I've edited for jewellery brands, food startups, dentists, real estate developers, content creators, and a guy who sells protein ramen. Yeah, it's been a range.


Here's what I do: I take your footage and make it worth watching. Cuts, pacing, colour, the whole edit that's my job. I'm just really, really good at my piece.


If your footage is sitting on a hard drive doing nothing, let's fix that.

What I Do

THE EDIT.
NOTHING ELSE.

01
Video Editing
Cuts, pacing, transitions, structure turning raw footage into something people actually want to watch. Reels, shorts, YouTube long-format, brand films, talking heads. If it needs editing, this is the spot.
02
Color Grading
The difference between footage that looks shot and footage that looks made. Mood, tone, consistency colour grading is the finishing layer that makes everything feel intentional and professional.
03
Sound Design
Audio mixing, levels, music sync, ambient sound making sure your video sounds as good as it looks. Sometimes the edit is perfect but the audio kills it. Not on my watch.

A note on scope: Video editing is part of your content, not all of it. I handle the edit; I don't write scripts, build content strategies, or design graphics. If someone's pitching you "video editor + social strategist + copywriter + designer" as one package that's four jobs. I do one, and I do it well.
